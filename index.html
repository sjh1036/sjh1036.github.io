Cognitive Games in VR: Team Lead/Developer

The "Cognitive Games in VR" project consists of two cognitive tests, the "N-Back" and "Tower of London" tests, designed and developed by our team for the purpose of studying the the correlation between physical balance and cognitive function in VR.

Our project followed Agile methodologies in our development cycles in order to provide regular and meaningful progress to our sponsor, the MOVE Lab of UNH, meaning my responsibilities as team lead were to facilitate communication with the sponsor as well as division of labor amongst the group. My responsibilities as a developer were within the design of the "N-Back Test" within the Unity 3D environment as well as the functionality of the test itself and its accompanying scripts.

The project was awarded First Place in CS: Applications as the UNH Undergraduate Research conference.
